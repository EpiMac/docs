In this documentation, we will embark on a journey into the world of Asahi Linux, an open-source project dedicated to bringing the Linux operating system to Apple Silicon devices. Whether you're already experienced with Linux or just beginning your exploration of open-source development, this guide is designed to provide you with the knowledge and tools needed to delve into Asahi Linux.
